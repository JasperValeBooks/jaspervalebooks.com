Coming Soon

Step back into the darkness that predates the Hollow—a time when the world still trembled in innocence, when shadows first learned to whisper, and when humanity’s steps faltered on the edge of a single, fatal choice.

Before the Hollow: The Birth of Sin delves into the ancient story of how light gave way to shadow, and how a single act of rebellion fractured paradise, setting in motion the haunting need for redemption. With the same atmospheric prose and haunting detail that brought Three Days Hollow to life, this prequel invites you to witness the cosmic conflict that unfolded in Eden’s twilight—the first battle between light and dark.

Journey through temptation’s web, feel the weight of innocence lost, and discover the origins of the Hollow—a realm shaped by humanity’s fall, yet destined for salvation’s dawn. Before the Hollow: The Birth of Sin is a tale of beauty corrupted, hope hidden in the ashes, and the eternal whisper that even in the darkest garden, a promise awaits.

Prepare to walk the shadowed paths that led to the Hollow. The beginning is coming soon.

Recent Publishing

Three Days Hollow is a tale whispered across the boundary between life and death. In a realm where silence reigns and shadows linger, this standalone novel unearths the hidden war waged between light and darkness. A reckoning awaits within the hollow.

Through a vivid blend of atmosphere and allegory, this novel leads readers into a liminal space—between crucifixion and resurrection—where souls are sifted, hope is tested, and eternity listens for a cry from the depths. Jasper Vale weaves a story that is both haunting and healing, an exploration of unseen battles and sacred silences.

Fans of speculative fiction, spiritual allegory, and deeply reflective storytelling will find Three Days Hollow to be a rare and resonant journey. It invites the reader to peer into the hollow places of the soul—and listen closely to what echoes back.

Beyond the Vale

Welcome to the Journey

Welcome to a realm where fiction pulses with the rhythm of mystery and revelation. Here, tales are not merely told—they are unearthed from the depths of silence, crafted with shadow and starlight, and written across the unseen boundaries between life and death.

This sanctuary of words is for the wanderers, the questioners, and the ones who sense that truth often wears a veil. Whether you seek the flicker of hope in darkness or echoes of eternity whispered through the veil, you will find yourself among kindred spirits.

Step beyond the veil. Embrace the unknown. Let the hollow speak—and listen closely, for its story is waiting for you to hear it.

About the Book

In a realm where silence reigns and shadows linger, Three Days Hollow unearths the hidden war waged between darkness and light. This standalone tale invites you into the hollow space between death and resurrection—where nothing is as final as it seems.

"What lies beyond death isn’t an end—it’s a reckoning."

Themes & Symbols

The Key: Symbol of truth and access, the key represents the knowledge hidden in plain sight, waiting for hearts brave enough to open the unseen.

The Crown: A costly emblem of authority and sacrifice, echoing the unseen ruler who reigns not by force, but through willing surrender.

The Hollow: A liminal space—between death and resurrection, silence and song—where the soul confronts eternity and faces the consequences of its actions among the living.

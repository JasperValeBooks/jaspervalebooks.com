A Story Your Church Won’t Forget

Dear Leader in Christ,

Grace and peace to you in Christ.

My name is Jasper Vale, and I’m reaching out as a fellow believer and storyteller. I’ve recently written a biblically-inspired novel titled Three Days Hollow, and I believe it could be a meaningful addition to your church’s library, small group resources, or even as a conversation starter around the power of Christ’s resurrection.

The story explores the unseen spiritual realm during the three days between Christ’s death and resurrection—drawing on Scripture, imagination, and reverence for the Word. It’s not only a fictional account, but a devotional experience designed to draw readers deeper into awe and wonder at Christ’s sacrifice and victory.

I’ve poured a great deal of heart, prayer, and research into this book—not only as a creative offering to the Lord, but as part of my effort to support my family through meaningful, purpose-driven work. Every purchase of the book helps build a future of stability and continued creative ministry for us, and I’m deeply grateful for every bit of encouragement or support.

If the story resonates with you, I’d love to connect further about:

  • Bulk orders for churches or book clubs
  • Discussion guides for ministry groups
  • Future devotional and study resources built around the book’s themes

Thank you for your time and the kingdom work you do daily. I’d be honored to hear your thoughts.

In Christ’s service,

Jasper Vale

Author of Three Days Hollow
